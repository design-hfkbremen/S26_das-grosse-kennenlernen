## Exercise 1 [10:30–11:00]
Let’s crowdsource our views on feedback in [this document](https://hfkbremen.sharepoint.com/:w:/s/DasgroeKennenlernenProjektgruppeStammtischSoSe26/IQBInenVAkZWQ5jqia-mqdTMAe8_O9djj6VLMruQfwSmKQI?e=QQCX1x): 

1. What is feedback useful for? What kind of feedback is most useful?  
2. What kind of feedback do you want / need?
3. Which feedback helped you the most? From whom, when and why did you learn the most?
4. From whom do you need feedback?
5. How soft / harsh do you want your feedback to be?
6. How does feedback change along the process? In what stage do you need feedback?
7. What helps you taking in feedback? When do you get defensive?
8. What helps you give feedback to someone else? What do you need to see / hear / know to give good feedback?
9. When don’t you want feedback?
