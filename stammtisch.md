# Stammtisch / Regular’s Table

The _Stammtisch / Regulars’ Table_ takes place every Thursday – open to anyone interested. 
Sharing a meal, inputs, and open discussions on (graphic) design, and visual culture create an informal space for exchange, inspiration, and getting to know each other.

Each _Stammtisch_ is self-organized by 2-3 students.

### Time / Location
- THU / 17–19:00 / C.A.T.E. (Segment 4 / EG)

### To Do for each _Stammtisch_
- Inspiration-Slot [10min]
- Topic [1h40]
- Announcement _(at least on the Tuesday prior)_
  - Poster
  - Instagram _(e.g. Story)_
- Snacks
