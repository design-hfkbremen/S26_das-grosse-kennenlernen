## Morphological Box [30min]
What possible ideas, variations or parameters are you considering in your project?
Let’s try them out with quick sketches and a systematic aproach: 

1. What questions and decisions are you currently struggeling with? Break down your project into different parameters, e.g.
   - focus
   - visual 
   - format
3. Now write all possible values of those parameters, e.g.:
   - focus: visual research | theoretical | historic | personal | documentary | speculative | participatory | ...
   - visual: photo | video | illustration | graphic | archive material | 3D | ...
   - format: print | web | poster | movie | exhibition | workshop | ...
3. Now select one value of each parameter. Try to create as many variations as possible. Max. 5mins / sketch [pen, paper, concept sentence].
