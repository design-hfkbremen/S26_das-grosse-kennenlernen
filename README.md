## Project Group – Supervision of independent & applied projects
The _Project Group_ is a practice-oriented format for self-initiated and applied design projects. Do you have your own project that you need feedback on? Do you know anyone who needs a poster? And under what conditions do we want to design?

### For whom?
For students who need feedback on their own projects or who want to team up with others for independent projects. Ideally, you already have a real-life design task in mind—whether it’s a logo for an association or your own website. Alternatively, there may also be opportunities for new projects.
Instead of designing and struggling alone, let’s collaborate, exchange feedback, and support each other. There will be experimental workshops on the topics of learning, studying, and working together, as well as input on practical topics such as invoices or terms and conditions.

### Do you need designers?
Students, initiatives, and departments at HfK are welcome to contact the _Project Group_ with design requests—e.g., for posters, publications, websites, logos, or other communication concepts.
We’re happy to discuss details, conditions and how collaboration, beyond a purely service-based relationship, could look like. Feel free to send us an email!

## Regulars’ Table – open to all
Accompanying this, the _Regulars’ Table_ takes place every Thursday–open to anyone interested. Sharing a meal, inputs, and open discussions on graphics, design, and visual culture create an informal space for exchange, inspiration, and getting to know each other.

## Additional Info
The _Regulars’ Table_ is open to anyone interested and takes place every Thursday 17–19:00 at C.A.T.E. / Segment 4.
The _Regulars’ Table_ is obligatory for students from the Project Group and my BA / MA colloquium.
The _Project Group_ runs continuously throughout Block A & B [09.04.–25.06.] and can be credited as a Block A course.
